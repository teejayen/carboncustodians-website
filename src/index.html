Zero-Waste Technology

Our advanced induction heating process transforms organic matter into pure carbon with zero flame and complete resource recovery.

Unlike conventional pyrolysis, our system requires no external energy after startup, recycling process gases to maintain optimal temperatures in a completely self-sustaining cycle.

Every component is captured and transformed into valuable outputs, creating a truly circular system with permanent environmental benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is biochar?

Biochar is a carbon-rich material produced through pyrolysis of organic matter in an oxygen-limited environment. It permanently sequesters carbon while providing numerous benefits for soil health and agriculture.

How does your pyrolysis technology work?

Our technology uses advanced induction heating to transform organic waste into biochar without flame. The process is self-sustaining, recapturing and utilising process gases to maintain optimal temperatures.

Do you sell or lease your equipment?

We primarily offer flexible leasing arrangements for our equipment, providing full technical support, maintenance, and operational assistance as part of our service.

What types of waste can your system process?

Our systems can process a wide range of organic materials including green waste, agricultural residues, wood waste, and many difficult-to-process materials that conventional systems cannot handle.

How can I generate carbon credits with your technology?

Biochar production creates opportunities for carbon credit generation through platforms like Puro.Earth. We provide comprehensive support for documentation, verification, and certification to help you maximise this additional revenue stream.
